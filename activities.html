Activities


Flood Area Cleaning Volunteer Program (Community Service)

Participated in a community clean-up program to help residents after a flood that was held by UiTM Kedah under co-curriculum club (Kesatria Kembara). It was located in Gurun, Kedah. This activity involved cleaning public areas, schools, organizing donations, and assisting affected families in restoring their homes and schools. It strengthened my teamwork, empathy, and sense of social responsibility.


River Roadtrip with Classmates

As part of a recreational and team-building activity. The trip provided an opportunity to strengthen friendships, improve teamwork and enhance communication skills through shared activities and cooperation. This experience also encouraged stress relief, mutual support, and a positive learning environment outside the classroom.
